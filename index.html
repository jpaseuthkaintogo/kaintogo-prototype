Our Story

How it all started

In 2025, we visited local bakeries and restaurants across Manila. At closing time, we saw trays of fresh food being discarded — not because it wasn't good, but because it hadn't sold.

For shops, it meant lost revenue. For the city, it meant unnecessary waste.

We realized something simple: The problem wasn't food. It was distribution.

KainToGo was built to fix that.
